Main Characters

Shinnosuke Nohara (Shinchan)

A funny 5-year-old kid. He is naughty but very kind sometimes.

Misae Nohara (Mom)

Shinchan's mom. She often gets angry, but she cares a lot about family.

Hiroshi Nohara (Dad)

Shinchan's father. He works in an office and likes relaxing after work.

Himawari (Sister)

The baby sister. She likes shiny things and cute stuff.

Shiro (Nohara family dog)

The family dog. Looks small but is smart and loyal.

Toru Kazama

A smart friend who is serious most of the time.

Masao Sato

A shy but good friend. He is easy to scare but very friendly.
